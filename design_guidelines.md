# Orbtrix Space Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
Premium minimalist aerospace aesthetic inspired by SpaceX - clean, precise, cinematic with a futuristic white theme.

## Brand Identity
**Color Palette:**
- Base: White and very light grey
- Accent: Teal-turquoise gradient (matching provided Orbtrix logos)
- Use teal for underlines, buttons, and highlights

**Logo Usage:**
- Use provided teal gradient Orbtrix logos (both symbol and full logo)
- Place full-text logo left in navbar
- Ensure high-quality rendering on light backgrounds

## Typography
- Bold, crisp typography creating a cinematic aerospace feeling
- Clear hierarchy: Large impactful headlines, medium subheadlines, clean body text
- Precise and professional tone throughout

## Layout System
**Spacing:** Use Tailwind units of 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, and 24 for consistent rhythm

**Structure:**
- Multi-page navigation (8 separate pages, not single scroll)
- Full-width sections with contained content areas
- Sticky transparent white navbar that darkens slightly on scroll
- Active navigation link highlighted with teal underline

## Navigation
- Sticky top navbar: white transparent background
- Orbtrix full logo left, menu links right
- Active state: teal underline
- Smooth scroll behavior

## Components

**Hero Sections:**
- Full-width cinematic satellite/Earth imagery backgrounds
- Large bold headlines with supporting subheadlines
- Dual CTAs with primary teal button + secondary outline button
- Buttons on images: blurred background for text legibility

**Product Cards:**
- Clean white cards with subtle shadows
- Hover animations (lift effect)
- Clear product titles and descriptions
- Links to dedicated product pages

**Forms (Contact Page):**
- Light card style
- Fields: Name, Organization, Email, Message
- Teal submit button
- Clean, spacious layout

**Info Blocks:**
- Icon + headline + description format
- White background with teal accent icons
- Used for About pillars and Solutions sections

## Animations & Effects
- Subtle parallax motion on background images (Earth/satellite on Home)
- Smooth reveal animations on scroll
- Card hover effects (gentle lift)
- Keep animations subtle and professional

## Images

**Home Page:**
- Hero: Cinematic view of Earth from orbit with satellite render overlay
- Full-width, high-resolution
- Parallax effect applied

**Product Pages:**
- Each product page has full hero banner with relevant satellite/space imagery

- Videra: Satellite camera perspective view

**Products Overview:**
- Background strip images for each product section
- High-quality satellite and space imagery

**Solutions Page:**
- Subtle satellite background texture (low opacity)

## Responsive Behavior
- Fully responsive across all devices
- Mobile: Stack navigation, single-column layouts
- Tablet/Desktop: Multi-column grids for product cards and info blocks
- Maintain cinematic feel at all viewport sizes

## Performance
- Optimize satellite imagery for fast loading
- Smooth scrolling performance
- SEO-friendly structure
